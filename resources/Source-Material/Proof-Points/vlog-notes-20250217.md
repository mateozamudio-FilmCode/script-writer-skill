## DAILY CAPTURE TEMPLATE

Use this same structure every day. Change the numbers, change the focus segment, but keep the rhythm consistent. The consistency of the daily capture is what makes the final edited piece powerful — the audience will see the progression from chaos to system.

**Every day, answer these three questions on camera:**

1. Where are we? (Numbers, current state, what's live, what's broken)
2. What am I building? (Show the work — screen, code, interviews, frameworks)
3. What did I learn? (One thought. Not a lecture. A seed.)

That's the vlog. Three questions. Every day. The rest is edit.
